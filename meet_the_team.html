Team Hydra

Hi! We are the team behind the hydra proshtetic arm. We are a motivated team of undergraduate, and now recently graduated, Computer Science and Computer Science and Engineering Students who are passionate about learning, building, and making the world better. Separately, our team carries vastly diverse strengths and weaknesses, but together we make an awesome cohesive team.

The thought of Hydra came to us around Spring 2015. From day 1 we knew we had a lot of work on our hands and with the budget of our own back pockets we faced it head on. Through tireless nights, subtle frustration, endless discussions, and incredible learning curves, we were able to make our idea tangible and actually come to life.
